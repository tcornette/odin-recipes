Garlic Breaded Chicken

This garlic breaded chicken recipe is quick, easy and delicious! Heres the ingredients list:

  • Chicken breast tenderloins
  • 1/4 cup of garlic salt
  • 3 cups of bread crumbs
  • 1 cup melted butter

Instructions:

  1. Mix together the breadcrumbs and garlic salt
  2. melt butter
  3. dip tenderloin in butter
  4. SThen dip tenderloin in breadcrumb/garlic salt mixture (cover completely)
  5. place on pan
  6. repeat untill all tenderloins are used
  7. You can pour remaining bread/garlic mixture onto chicken
  8. Bake at 350 for around 20 minutes, ensure chicken reaches 165 degrees F
