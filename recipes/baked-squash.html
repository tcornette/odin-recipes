Easy Baked Squash

This baked squash is so easy and it tastes grest! Heres the ingredients list:

  • 1 Squash
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic salt

Instructions:

  1. Slice the squash into slices 1/4 inch thick
  2. Toss the slices in olive oil
  3. place slices on pan
  4. Sprinkle a liberal amount of garlic salt on the slices of squash
  5. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes or untill edges are crispy
