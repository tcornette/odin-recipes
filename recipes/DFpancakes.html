Dairy Free Pancakes

These dairy free pancakes are just as good as the real ones! Heres the ingredients list:

  • 1.5 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/8 cup of sugar
  • 1 tablepoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1.5 cups of milk alternative
  • 3 tablepoons canola oil
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Mix together all ingredients untill smooth
  2. Heat a skillet to medium heat
  3. Spray pan with oil (before each pancake)
  4. Scoop 1/3 cup of mix onto skillet
  5. When bubbles appear accross the whole pancake, flip it!
  6. repeat untill all batter is used
